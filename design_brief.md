NEXUSX WEBSITE DESIGN BRIEF

(For Website Designer / UI Designer / Webflow Designer)

1. What This Website Is (And Is NOT)
This website is NOT:

❌ a logistics company website
❌ a shipping/export trading site
❌ a cargo or supply chain platform

Do NOT use:

• cargo ships
• containers
• warehouses
• ports
• logistics icons

This website IS:

A modern global business platform that represents manufacturers internationally.

Think of it as closer to:

• Stripe
• Linear
• Apple enterprise pages
• McKinsey Digital

The company helps manufacturers expand globally through structured representation and sales engagement, called Supplier Partnering.

The website should communicate:

Global Growth Without Export Complexity

The brochure emphasizes that the real challenge for manufacturers is global execution, not product quality, and NexusX provides the structured representation needed for international markets.

2. Website Objective

The website must achieve 3 strategic goals.

1️⃣ Explain the Model

Supplier Partnering is not widely understood.

The website must clearly explain:

• how NexusX works
• why this model is better than traditional exporting

2️⃣ Build Trust Quickly

Manufacturers must feel:

• this company is serious
• this company understands global markets
• this is a professional representation partner

Leadership experience and international exposure mentioned in the brochure should support credibility.

3️⃣ Reduce Perceived Risk

Manufacturers should feel:

• they can expand globally without building export teams
• this is a structured, low-risk model
